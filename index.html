Welcome!

Welcome to my academic homepage! I am a Ph. D. scholar at Banaras Hindu University (BHU) working in the group of Prof. Devendra Kumar Mishra, focusing on quantum optics, nonlinear interactions, and quantum metrology. This page highlights my research, publications, and professional journey.

My work focuses on quantum optics, nonlinear and interferometric metrology using nonclassical lights. I've collaborated with international labs, including those at the University of Hamburg and Max Planck Institute. Broadly, I am interested in quantum metrology, two-photon absorption, and the design of quantum-enhanced optical setups.

Research Interests

  • Quantum interferometry and metrology
  • Non-degenerate two-photon absorption
  • Nonlinear quantum optics
  • Squeezed light, Schrödinger-cat states
  • Quantum information and sensing
